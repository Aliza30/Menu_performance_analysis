## Strategic Recommendations

### 1. Fix The Weekend Problem
- Launch **weekend-only combo deals**
- Introduce **Saturday Italian brunch menu**
- Run **loyalty double points on weekends**

### 2. Push Italian & Asian Premium Hard
- Introduce 1–2 new Italian items above $18
- Add **chef's special Asian tasting plate** at $20+
- Train staff to upsell Mid → Premium on these two categories

### 3. Kill The Wednesday Slump
- **"Wednesday Wins"** discounted premium item promotion
- Corporate lunch package for nearby offices
- Flash deals on social media every Wednesday morning

### 4. Unlock Mexican Premium
- Mexican has **zero Premium revenue** — that is an open opportunity
- Introduce wagyu tacos, birria, upscale enchiladas at $16–18
- Test for 30 days and measure

### 5. Cut The Budget Menu
- Budget tier = 12% of revenue with ~40% kitchen complexity
- Reduce to **2 items max** — fries + one appetizer
- Redirect kitchen bandwidth to premium execution

## Conclusion

> The restaurant has a **strong, profitable core** — Italian Premium and Asian Premium are genuinely exceptional performers. The problem isn't the food, it's the **menu bloat, weekend neglect, and underpriced Mexican category** holding the ceiling down.
>
> Fix those three things with discipline over 90 days and **$180,000–$200,000 quarterly revenue is realistic** — a 12–25% uplift without adding a single new customer.
